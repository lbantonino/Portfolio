ABOUT

Welcome to my portfolio! I am Antonino Lo Bianco, a Junior Full-Stack web developper specialised in frontend. I'm currently training to be a junior web developer at BeCode in Brussels (Belgium), where I'm immersed in the vast world of full-stack MERN (MongoDB, Express, React, NodeJS, of course including Html, CSS (Sass, Tailwind) and JavaScript). My passion for web development, and programming in particular, began during my web development classes at Saint-Luc high school. Combined with my proactivity and autonomy, this passion has propelled me towards mastery of modern web technologies.

Currently, while immersing myself in learning web programming languages (such as JavaScript with React and NodeJS, PHP with Laravel, as well as MySQL), I'm also actively engaged in mobile development using React Native. With a view to specialising further in backend and object-oriented programming (OOP), I'm devoting myself exclusively to learning the Java programming language. My website is where I present my web development projects and showcase my future mobile development projects.

My academic career began with a CESS diploma with a qualification in computer graphics in June 2015 at Institut Saint-Luc in Brussels completed by a bachelor's degree in advertising at ESA Saint-Luc in Brussels in 2018. These experiences have given me a solid grounding in the field of visual communication.

As well as my academic background, my professional career has included various roles such as Marketing Director at Crispin Medical and Sales Director at VOO (a Belgian telephone operator). These experiences have forged skills in marketing strategy, sales, communication, negotiation and operational management, strengthening my ability to tackle challenges creatively.

Feel free to explore the various sections to find out more about my skills, projects and discoveries. If you have any questions or would like to work with me, please don't hesitate to contact me.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio!
