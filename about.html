ABOUT

Welcome to my Portfolio! I am Antonino Lo Bianco and I am a Junior Full Stack web developper specialized in Backend.
I am currently undergoing training as a junior web developer at BeCode in Brussels (Belgium) , where I delve into the vast world of the MERN Fullstack (MongoDB, Express, React, NodeJS, obviously including Html, CSS (Sass, Tailwind) and JavaScript). My passion for web development, particularly programming, began during my web development courses at Saint-Luc secondary school. Combined with my proactivity and autonomy, this passion propels me towards mastering modern web technologies.

Currently, while immersing myself in the learning of web programming languages (such as JavaScript with React and NodeJS, PHP with Laravel, as well as MySQL), I am also actively engaged in mobile development using React Native. With the aim of further specializing in backend and object-oriented programming (OOP), I am exclusively dedicating myself to learning the Java programming language. My website serves as a space where I showcase my web development projects and outline my future plans for mobile development.


My academic journey commenced with obtaining a bachelor's degree in advertising from ESA Saint-Luc in Brussels in 2018, complemented by earning a CESS diploma with a qualification in infographics in June 2015 at the Saint-Luc Institute in Brussels. These experiences provided me with a solid foundation in the field of visual communication.

Beyond my academic background, my professional journey has included various roles such as marketing director at Crispin Medical and sales manager at VOO (phone operator). These experiences have forged skills in marketing strategy, sales, communication, negotiation, and operational management, enhancing my ability to approach challenges with creativity.

Feel free to explore the different sections to learn more about my skills, projects, and discoveries. For any questions or collaboration proposals, please don't hesitate to contact me.
